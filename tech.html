Welcome to My Profile

My name is Narendra Damodardas Modi, born on 17 September 1950. I became the Prime Minister of India in May 2014 and was re-elected in 2019. Before that, I served as the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014. I represent the Varanasi constituency in Parliament. Some of the initiatives I launched include Digital India, Make in India, and Atmanirbhar Bharat.
